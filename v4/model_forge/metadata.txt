It reads your installed algorithms and their parameter signatures, inspects loaded layers (fields, CRS, raster metadata), and uses that context to produce more tailored models. Supports multiple APIs. Includes an option for two-phase generation (plan then code), color-coded editable model preview, and automatic validation with LLM-assisted repair.
