about=Model Forge lets you describe a geoprocessing workflow in plain text and generates a ready-to-use QGIS Graphical Model (.model3) that you can immediately open, inspect and tweak in Model Designer. It recognises common GIS patterns (clip + dissolve + statistics, raster reclassification + zonal stats, buffer + intersection, etc.) and emits proper model definitions with inputs, algorithms and wired connections. Supports both local LLMs via Ollama and remote APIs (OpenAI-compatible). Models are saved to your Processing models folder and appear in the Toolbox instantly.
